Atty. Farhan “Far” Jumdain

Juris Doctor

Atty. Farhan “Far” Jumdain is a dedicated legal professional whose career reflects a deep commitment to public service, corporate excellence, and advocacy for migrant workers.

Born into a family rooted in service and discipline, Atty. Far is the son of Dr. Hji. Hassan Gabra Takaki Jumdain, DPA, a former Regional Director of OWWA Region IX and former Labor Attaché. Together with his mother, Seha T. Jumdain, a licensed Pharmacist, they instilled in him a strong sense of purpose, integrity, and perseverance.

Atty. Far earned his Bachelor’s degree in Legal Studies at the Lyceum of the University of the Philippines, and completed his Juris Doctor degree from Adamson University College of Law.

He currently serves as an OIC-Chief, Attorney at the Department of Migrant Workers National Capital Region, providing legal counsel for OFWs. Previously, he served as an OIC-Chief at the Government Procurement Policy Board – Technical Support Office (GPPB-TSO).

Beyond his legal career, Atty. Far is also a musician and bassist for Illiana (PH), a heavy metalcore band known for its original compositions. Music offers him a space for creative expression and balance.

Grounded by his upbringing, sharpened by experience, and driven by purpose, he now steps into a new role as a husband—ready to face life’s future chapters with love, loyalty, and unwavering strength.

Atty. Farhan Jumdain Status: Husband

Subject

The Bride

Ref No.

002-B

Aziza “Zee” Y. Amil

Registered Nurse

Graceful, grounded, and globally minded, Aziza “Zee” Y. Amil is a woman of purpose and compassion. Zee is the beloved daughter of the late Engr. Bobby Sakaluran Amil Al-Haj, MSE, and Hja. Samira Yusop Amil.

Zee earned her degree of BS in Nursing from WMSU. She began her career at Zamboanga City Medical Center (ZCMC), serving in key departments including OB-Gyne, Pediatrics, and Adult Medical, cultivating both skill and empathy.

She continued her service at Zamboanga Peninsula Medical Center before moving to Doha, Qatar, in 2017. She now works at the Surgical Specialty Center of Hamad Medical Corporation, contributing to a global mission of delivering safe, effective, and compassionate care.

Outside the hospital, Zee is a passionate artist and creative soul. She expresses herself through painting and music, blending colors and melodies to tell heartfelt stories. A talented singer-musician, she not only performs but also writes her own original compositions.

A true citizen of the world, her love for travel has taken her from Japan and Switzerland to Paris and Saudi Arabia. Whether in scrubs or stilettos, she is known for her effortless elegance and generous spirit.

She is the treasured eldest granddaughter of His Eminence Grand Mufti of Region IX Ust. Abdulgani Yusop. Now, with a full heart and steady faith, Zee is ready to begin a new chapter, a married life with Atty. Farhan Jumdain.

Aziza Y. Amil Status: Wife

How It All Began

A Simple “Hey” That Changed Everything (25 August 2024)

Like many great love stories, theirs began unexpectedly—with a single word: “Hey.” What started as a simple greeting turned into long video calls, late-night conversations, and a growing connection that neither of them saw coming. Far and Zee discovered shared values, dreams, and quirks. Each story they told, each laugh they shared, slowly wove together the threads of something beautiful.

Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar (26 October 2024)

After a month of exchanging messages and late-night video calls, Far brought Zee to Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar in Quezon City — a place rich with history, charm, and quiet corners for getting to know each other.
